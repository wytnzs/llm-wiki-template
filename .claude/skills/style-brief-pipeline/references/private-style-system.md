# Private Style System

Use this reference for the owner's own content production.

## Purpose

Private mode optimizes for best output, not neutrality. It may use the owner's long-term expression assets, preferred stance, recurring audience, and content rhythm.

The goal is not to make content generic. The goal is to make it recognizably thoughtful, useful, and credible.

## Mother Style

Use one stable mother style:

`克制知识编辑风 + 材质化解释图`

Meaning:

- Calm, precise, and credible.
- Strong viewpoint, but not sensational.
- Clean editorial layout for reading.
- Visuals explain relationships, not merely decorate.
- Use restraint as the high-end signal: fewer colors, fewer effects, sharper hierarchy.

## Voice Defaults

Use this voice unless a stronger personal IP card says otherwise:

- Speak to smart practitioners, not passive beginners.
- Lead with judgment, then explain the reasoning.
- Prefer concrete scenes, real constraints, and operational advice.
- Avoid empty encouragement, generic slogans, and exaggerated certainty.
- For insurance and dispute content, never promise outcomes.
- CTA should feel like an invitation to think or act, not a sales shout.

## Private Content Routes

### Deep Editorial

Use for观点文、行业趋势、方法论、案例拆解、个人创业复盘.

Style:

- restrained red/black/graphite or olive editorial
- numbered sections
- opening thesis or tension
- 1-3 underlined keywords per paragraph
- one strong quote or conclusion block only when needed

### Dense Tutorial

Use for教程、工具盘点、工作流、操作指南、AI工具实操.

Style:

- higher information density
- step labels, cards, comparison blocks
- screenshots or generated explanation images
- concrete "what to do next" sections

### Material Explainer

Use for小红书封面、轮播核心图、图表美化、公众号文中解释图.

Style:

- off-white or light gray background
- black ink outline
- soft gray/white material objects
- one accent color
- 3-5 short Chinese labels
- no dense paragraph text inside image

## Decision Priority

When constraints conflict:

1. Accuracy and legal boundary.
2. Reader usefulness.
3. Recognizable personal style.
4. Platform performance.
5. Decorative beauty.

Do not reverse this order.
